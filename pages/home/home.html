About Patch

From Old VFW to Your New Favorite Place

Located 25 minutes northeast of Charlottesville, we've transformed a historic VFW hall into 12 acres of good times. Patch-brewed beer on tap, our County Line Food Truck serving smash burgers daily, and more ways to unwind than you can count.
